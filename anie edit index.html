Welcome to CoreGem!

At you service 24/7 to make banking easier for you.

Read More

Internet Banking- Retail

Retail internet banking- web platform, to provide financial services to the general public,allows consumers to manage their money by giving them access to basic banking services, credit, and financial advice.

Internet Banking- Coporate

Coporate internet banking- web platform, to allow you carry out secure internet banking transactions and e-payments of all kinds, monitor trade transactions and administer payroll.

Mobile App & USSD

Mobile app and USSD banking- mobile, to provide you with banking services with the use of our mobile app or use of USSD codes on your mobile phones to make quick transactions.

Quick Summary

Open Architecture

All the banking capabilities are consumable and usable by external systems (OA).

SaaS Architecture

Each banking capability is available as a service to other banking capabilities within the stack (SOAS).

Driven Architecture

APIs expose the banking capabilities to drive the Open Architecture design for CoreGEM (API).

Information Security

Information Security is central from Channels to microservices to the core, ensuring confidentiality,consistency and integrity (Security).
